Nexi is what customers see. Nexus² is what makes it work. The brain is the same on every surface — only the shape changes.

## Six surfaces, one brain

### 1. Chat
The widget on your site, app or product. Lightweight, brand-tuned, and aware of the page the customer is on.

### 2. Voice
The same assistant on a phone line or in-product voice. Lower latency, the same answers.

### 3. Search
Replace the box that returns ten blue links with one that returns an answer — and cites the source.

### 4. Agents
Background workflows that take action — booking, escalating, summarising — instead of just talking.

### 5. API
For teams who want Nexi inside something they're already building.

### 6. Embed
Drop a Nexi panel into Notion, Slack, an admin tool. Anywhere your team already lives.

## Why this matters

> Customers don't think in channels. They think in goals.

A shopper who started on the website and called in shouldn't have to start over. A patient who asked a question in the app shouldn't have to repeat it to a nurse. One layer, one memory, every surface.

That's what Nexi is. Not a chatbot. A presence.
